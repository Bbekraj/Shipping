Sea Freight

Gurkha Shipping Line Pvt Ltd offers import and export containerized services for both full (FCL) and part load (LCL) cargo worldwide.

Customs Brokers

Gurkha Shipping Line offer professional customs clearance services for import or export consignments by land and sea at all major land and seaports. Our nationally licensed staff works closely with clients to help them avoid costly penalties and delays while the goods are under customs hold by ensuring fast and smooth clearance of cargo.

We offer a complete portfolio of brokerage services for both Import and Export custom clearance to our clients (including duty drawback, full product line classification, compliance management, duty reduction assistance, etc.). Our electronic interface with Nepali Customs and other government agencies through the Automated Broker Interface permits us to clear your shipment immediately on landing. This allows us to streamline the movement of your cargo and further reduce your overall cycle time.

Transportation service

    Some of our capabilities include:

  • Transportation of heavy and odd dimensional cargo from India to any destination in Nepal and vice-versa.
  • Project cargo movement from inception to erection and commission.
  • Delivery to consignee destination of import cargo after custom clearance from seaport on our own delivery trucks.
  • Personal baggage trucking to passenger doorstep after clearance.
  • Container transportation trailers for all inland destinations in Nepal and India.
  • Pickup of Export Cargo from shipper warehouse to nearest port.

With our expertise in road and rail logistics operations, we can offer optimum, reasonable, coordinated and competent transport solutions.
