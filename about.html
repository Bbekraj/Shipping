About Us

Gurkha Shipping line Pvt. Ltd. offers a whole set of freight forwarding solutions for both domestic and international customers. At Gurkha Shipping Line(GSPL), we deliver highly flexible services through our partnerships with leading shipping lines. The cultivation of long-term, secure partnerships with major shipping lines brings tangible benefits to our customers as we have reliable links with all commercial centres worldwide, which is why our Sea Freight services are among the best in the marketplace. Through these connections, we can guarantee capacity and also offer adaptability to the ever changing shipping regulations of every country and port, thus enabling a smooth passage for your consignment.We have logistic experts over 20 countries with additional agents located around the world. Hence, our specialists assist you identify the means of transportation that best fits your requirement with the quality of service you demand by us. In addition, our global experts can help you with import and export services, Air freight forwarding and Ocean freight forwarding (FCL & LCL) which leads to open up worldwide market place for you. We have been maintaining a comprehensive idea of transportation through air freight and sea freight. Our purchasing power and advanced technological systems leads to competitive rates, quick transit and a better view to your shipment which enhances supply chain performance.

The term logistics Management is that “Part of Supply Chain Management that plans, implements and controls the efficient, effective, forward and reverse flow and storage of goods, services and related information between the point of origin and the point of consumption in order to meet customer’s requirements. Therefore, in total, logistics is an art which requires lot of commitment and dedication. Gurkha Shipping line Private Ltd understands this very well and is committed in delivering the best solutions for an efficient supply chain. Gurkha Shipping line Pvt. Ltd. would like to position itself as a company “to be known and respected as the best solution provider with excellent customer service”. We have the expertise and knowledge in offering Door to Door Solutions as we always believe that we should make our customers to focus on their core competency area and leave the logistics challenges to us.
