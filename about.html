About Us

Birgunj Shipping line offers a whole set of freight forwarding solutions for both domestic and international customers. We have logistic experts over 20 countries with additional agents located around the world. Hence, our specialists assist you identify the means of transportation that best fits your requirement with the quality of service you demand by us. In addition, our global experts can help you with import and export services, Air freight forwarding and Ocean freight forwarding (FCL & LCL) which leads to open up worldwide market place for you. We have been maintaining a comprehensive idea of transportation through air freight and sea freight. Our purchasing power and advanced technological systems leads to competitive rates, quick transit and a better view to your shipment which enhances supply chain performance.

The term logistics Management is that “Part of Supply Chain Management that plans, implements and controls the efficient, effective, forward and reverse flow and storage of goods, services and related information between the point of origin and the point of consumption in order to meet customer’s requirements. Therefore, in total, logistics is an art which requires lot of commitment and dedication. Kronos Logistics India Private Ltd understands this very well and is committed in delivering the best solutions for an efficient supply chain. Kronos logistics India Private Ltd would like to position itself as a company “to be known and respected as the best solution provider with excellent customer service”. We have the expertise and knowledge in offering Door to Door Solutions as we always believe that we should make our customers to focus on their core competency area and leave the logistics challenges to us.
